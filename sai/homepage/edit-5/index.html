One Legacy, a million stories

The Sri Sathya Sai Central Trust was founded in 1972 by Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba as a public charitable trust with the objectives of extending relief to the poor, including education and health, and any other objects of general public utility as defined in section 2(15) of the Indian Income Tax Act of 1991, without any discrimination.

The People’s Trust

The greatest strength of the Sri Sathya Sai Central Trust is its people. The projects facilitated by The Trust brings together over 6,00,000 engaged individuals from diverse backgrounds under several categories - employees, volunteers, students, alumni and devotees ...

Our people are spread all over the world-from Patna to Peru, from Gujarat to Guatemala, from Bengal to Benghazi, in over 180 countries. From carpenters to doctors to executives, musicians, managers, entrepreneurs and they bring to the organisation diverse expertise.

This diverse intellectual and human capital comes together for a singular purpose to serve.
