Messaging

Real-time communication with team using private and group chat keeping all your team members up to date anywhere anytime.
Groups - 1:1 / Private / Public

  • Securely connect with a team member for a 1:1 chat

  • Private Group chat with members of the group

  • Collaborate with all members using Public groups

Threaded Messaging

  • Start a thread by replying to a particular message by simply clicking on the button next to a message in a room

  • Cut out the noise in the room while still maintaining simplicity

  • Have a structured conversation even when you have a lot going on in the room

@notifications

  • Personalize to get web, mobile or even an email alert

  • Configure to receive notifications only on mentions

  • Be updated on team conversation even on the go

Attachments - Images, Audio, Video, Documents

  • Attach and share any type of file

  • Move away from the clutter of email chains

  • Single platform for all communication types

Administration

  • Granular User & group management including SSO/SAML

  • Instant activation & deactivation of features

  • Easy and quick on-boarding process
