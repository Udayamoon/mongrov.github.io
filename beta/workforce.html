Workforce Management Solution

Mongrov’s Workforce Management Solution is a Collaboration and workforce tracking system. Mongrov employs powerful and cutting-edge technology to keep track of On-field workforce and vehicles along with real time communication with teams using messaging and conferencing. Keeping all your team members up to date anywhere anytime. Also engage remote employees, share content, schedule meetings and connect instantly.



It is a location-based service that runs as an app on employee smartphones and is administered by a manager from a browser-based Web tool. It solution gives small and medium-sized businesses the competitive edge by improving job scheduling, two-way communication with field workforce, streamlined paperwork and so much more. Your sales team and technicians location are continuously logged to provide accurate real time data. Painless to deploy, easy to use and affordable, It keep the office and the field connected and on task.

Benefits

  • Create schedules for thousands of staff, factoring tens of thousands of criteria, and distributes the schedules to everyone involved, in minutes
  • Respond quickly and easily to last minute requests using inbuilt communication platform
  • Reduce error-prone, time-consuming paperwork with digital tools
  • Easily message and call within the same platform
  • Start and finish more jobs with mobile tools that streamline processes
